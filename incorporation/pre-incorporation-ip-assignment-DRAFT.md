# PRE-INCORPORATION IP ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

**DRAFT FOR FOUNDER REVIEW — NOT LEGAL ADVICE. HAVE A LAWYER REVIEW BEFORE SIGNING.**

> **What this document is (plain English):** Dispango and CoverCraft were built *before* Jam Works Inc. existed — so right now, they legally belong to the individual people who wrote them, not to the company. This one-page agreement transfers all of that past work into the company on signing day. Without it, the company you just incorporated owns nothing, and any future investor's lawyer will find that in the first hour of due diligence. Items in `[SQUARE BRACKETS]` need filling in.

## 1. Assignment

For good and valuable consideration (including the issuance to each Assignor of founder shares in the Corporation, the receipt and sufficiency of which are acknowledged), each Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns to the Corporation all of their worldwide right, title, and interest in and to the intellectual property described in Schedule A, including: all software source code, prompts, configurations, and documentation; all copyright, trade secrets, know-how, and inventions (whether or not patentable); all product names, brands, logos, and associated goodwill; all domain names, accounts, and registrations; and all causes of action for past infringement.

## 2. Moral rights

Each Assignor waives, in favour of the Corporation and its successors, all moral rights in the assigned works.

## 3. Further assurances

Each Assignor shall promptly sign any further documents and do anything else reasonably necessary to perfect the Corporation's ownership (including transferring account credentials, domain registrations, and third-party service accounts listed in Schedule A).

## 4. Representations

Each Assignor represents that, to their knowledge: (a) they have the right to assign what this Agreement assigns; (b) the assigned IP was not created in the course of, or using the resources of, any employer, and is not subject to any prior employer's invention-assignment or similar agreement; and (c) they have not previously licensed or encumbered the assigned IP.

> **PLAIN ENGLISH — Section 4:** This is each founder personally promising "my old/current employer has no claim on this code." This is why each of you must actually re-read your employment contract before signing — you are warranting it's clean.

## 5. General

Governed by the laws of Alberta and the federal laws of Canada. May be signed in counterparts, including electronically. This Agreement survives and is confirmed by the Founders' and Shareholders' Agreement of even date.
