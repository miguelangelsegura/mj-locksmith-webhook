# SHARE SUBSCRIPTIONS

**JAM WORKS INC.** (the "Corporation")

**DRAFT FOR FOUNDER REVIEW — NOT LEGAL ADVICE. HAVE A LAWYER REVIEW BEFORE SIGNING.**

> **What this is (plain English):** three short, near-identical forms — one per founder — each being that founder formally saying "I'm buying 1,000,000 shares for $10, here's my money." It's the paperwork that turns the directors' *decision* to issue shares (in the resolutions) into each founder actually *owning* them. One page each, signed by the buyer.

> **PLAIN ENGLISH.** Each founder signs their own block and pays their $10 (an e-transfer to the company account is perfect). Keep proof of the three payments with these forms in the minute book — that's what makes the shares "fully paid," which matters later for the lifetime capital gains exemption and for clean investor due diligence. After signing, the officers record all three of you in the securities register and (optionally) issue share certificates.
