FAQ

Questions, answered

Does it use my own phone number?

Yes. You forward your existing line to Dispango, so the number on your truck and your ads stays exactly the same.

Will it sound like a robot?

No. It uses a natural, conversational voice and asks questions like a real receptionist would. Most callers never realize they're talking to AI.

What does it cost?

A simple flat monthly plan — no per-call charges. Book a quick demo and we'll walk you through pricing for your shop.

What happens to my callers' information?

It's used only to send you the lead and is stored securely. We follow Canadian privacy rules (PIPEDA). See our privacy policy.

What if I'm available to answer myself?

Dispango only steps in when you can't get to the phone — busy, after hours, or no answer. When you pick up, it stays out of the way.
