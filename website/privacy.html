Privacy Policy

Last updated: —. This is a starting template — have it reviewed before you rely on it.

Dispango (Dispango, "we", "us") provides an AI call-answering and lead-dispatch service for locksmith businesses. This policy explains what we collect and how we handle it, in line with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

What we collect

When someone calls a business that uses Dispango, our system may capture the caller's phone number, name, service address, and details of their request, along with a recording/transcript and summary of the call.

Why we collect it

Solely to deliver the lead to the locksmith business the caller contacted, and to operate and improve the service. We do not sell personal information.

How it's stored and shared

Information is stored securely and shared only with the locksmith business that received the call. We retain it only as long as needed for that purpose.

Your choices

To access, correct, or delete information we hold about you, or to ask a question, contact us at our email.
